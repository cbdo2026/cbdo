XXII Brazilian Colloquium on Orbital Dynamics

Venue

The XXII Brazilian Colloquium on Orbital Dynamics will take place at Centro Universitário Geraldo Di Biase (UGB), in Volta Redonda, Rio de Janeiro, Brazil.

How to Get to Volta Redonda

Volta Redonda is conveniently located between Rio de Janeiro and São Paulo, with easy access by car and intercity bus.

From Rio de Janeiro

  • By car: Approximately 2 h 13 min (129 km) via Rodovia Presidente Dutra (BR-116).
  • By bus: Operated by Cidade do Aço. See website.

From São Paulo

  • By car: Approximately 4 h 7 min (327 km) via BR-116.
  • By bus: Operated by Viação Cometa. See website.

Accommodation

The CBDO 2026 recommends the following hotels in Volta Redonda, both located close to the event venue:

  • Seleto Hotel — approximately 11 minutes by car from the venue. For reservations, visit the hotel website or call (24) 3343-3349.
  • Vila Business Hotel — approximately 9 minutes by car from the venue. For reservations, visit the hotel website or call (24) 3340-2700.

Additional accommodation options are available throughout the city, including more budget-friendly alternatives.

Public Transportation

The local bus fare in Volta Redonda is R$ 5.90.

For public transit routes and schedules, you may use Moovit or Cittamobi , available on Android and iOS.

Additional information on bus schedules can be found on the Volta Redonda City Hall website .
