XXII Brazilian Colloquium on Orbital Dynamics

Venue

The XXII Brazilian Colloquium on Orbital Dynamics will take place at Centro Universitário Geraldo Di Biase (UGB/FERP), in Volta Redonda, Rio de Janeiro, Brazil.

Address: Rua Deputado Geraldo Di Biase, 81, Aterrado, Volta Redonda - RJ, 27213-080

Phone: +55 (24) 3345-1700

Access: Entrance B

Room: Block 4, 6th Floor, Milton Carlos Auditorium

How to Get to Volta Redonda

Volta Redonda is conveniently located between Rio de Janeiro and São Paulo, with easy access by car and intercity bus.

From Rio de Janeiro

  • By car: Approximately 2 h 13 min (129 km) via Rodovia Presidente Dutra (BR-116).
  • By bus: Operated by Cidade do Aço. See website.

From São Paulo

  • By car: Approximately 4 h 7 min (327 km) via BR-116.
  • By bus: Operated by Viação Cometa. See website.
