XXII Brazilian Colloquium on Orbital Dynamics

First Circular — 7 April 2026

The XXIII Brazilian Colloquium on Orbital Dynamics (CBDO) will be held from December 7 to 11, 2026, at Centro Universitário Geraldo Di Biase (UGB) in Volta Redonda, Rio de Janeiro, Brazil.

The event will bring together researchers, students, and professionals in the fields of Celestial Mechanics, Orbital Dynamics, Planetary Science, Space Dynamics and Control, and Nonlinear Dynamical Systems.

The Organizing Committee invites the community to complete the pre-registration form to assess interest in participating in the event. At this stage, there is no need to submit an abstract. Only those who complete the form will receive subsequent circulars.
