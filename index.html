About the Event

The Brazilian Colloquium on Orbital Dynamics (CBDO) is the most traditional South American event dedicated to Celestial Mechanics, Orbital Dynamics, Planetary Science, Fundamental Astronomy, Space Dynamics and Control, and Nonlinear Dynamical Systems.

With 44 years of history and held biennially, CBDO brings together researchers, students, and professionals from Brazil and abroad, fostering scientific exchange and collaboration. The programme features invited plenary talks by international speakers, as well as oral presentations, poster sessions, short courses, and round tables.

Recognised for its dynamic and collaborative environment, CBDO provides a unique forum for discussing recent advances and developing new ideas in the field.

The Organising Committee invites the community to participate and submit contributions aligned with the event’s topics.
